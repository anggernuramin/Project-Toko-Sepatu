About

AnaShoes was introduced in 2023 by Angger Nur Amin, the owner of a vulcanized shoe factory located in East Java. AnaShoes offers a wide range of types and models suitable for various activities. Each pair of AnaShoes goes through a long and detailed process, starting from material selection, production processes, to rigorous quality inspections. This is done to ensure that the quality of AnaShoes is well-maintained. With abundant resources, AnaShoes is capable of producing shoes in large quantities and of the highest quality, allowing people from all walks of life to afford high-quality footwear at an affordable price.
